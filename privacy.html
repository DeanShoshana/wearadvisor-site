Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

WearAdvisor uses weather and location information to recommend outfits.

Information Used

  • Location: used to request a local forecast when you choose current-location weather.
  • Selected cities: used to request weather for travel or planned locations.
  • Preferences: name, age, comfort settings, units, style preference, and reminder settings are stored on device.
  • Purchase status: checked with Apple StoreKit to unlock premium access.

Weather Providers

The app uses Apple WeatherKit where available. It may use Open-Meteo as a fallback weather provider. When a fallback provider is used, coordinates for the selected weather location may be sent to that provider to retrieve a forecast.

Storage

Preferences are stored locally on the device using app storage. The app does not require an account or login.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app owner before release through the support address configured in App Store Connect.
